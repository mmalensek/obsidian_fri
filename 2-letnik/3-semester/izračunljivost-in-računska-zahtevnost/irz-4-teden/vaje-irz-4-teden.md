### Vsebina:

Regularni izrazi in njihov jezik:
- $\emptyset$ in $\emptyset$
- $\varepsilon$ in $\{\varepsilon\}$
- $a \in \Sigma$ in $\{a\}$
- $R_1 + R_2$ in $L(R_1) \cup L(R_2)$
- $R_1 R_2$ in $L(R_1)L(R_2)$
- $R_1^*$ in $(L(R_1))^*$
- $L_1L_2 = \{uv \mid u \in L_1, v \in L_2\}$
- $L^* = \{\varepsilon\} \cup L \cup LL \cup LLL \cup ...$
